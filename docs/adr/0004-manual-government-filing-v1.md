# ADR 0004: Government filing remains manual in V1

Status: accepted — 14 July 2026

## Decision

firmOS prepares GST and ITR workpapers and filing packs. The CA manually signs in, completes CAPTCHA/OTP/EVC/DSC/payment/submission, and uploads acknowledgement evidence.

## Consequences

- V1 does not claim portal automation, automatic statutory filing or automatic tax payment.
- Workflows stop at deterministic manual hand-off points.
- Any future portal automation needs legal, security, provider and operating approval through a new ADR.
